Hufnagel Equal-Area Transformation

Herbert Hufnagel introduced a generalization of the Mollweide projection, resulting in a family of equal-area projections. The Mollweide, Eckert Ⅳ and Wagner Ⅳ projections are members of this family.

All projections are equal-area. Some paramter combinations create folding graticules.

Source: Hufnagel, H. 1989. Ein System unecht-zylindrischer Kartennetze für Erdkarten. Kartographische Nachrichten, 39(3), 89–96.
