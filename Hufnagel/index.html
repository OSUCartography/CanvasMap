Hufnagel Equal-Area Transformation

Hufnagel, H. 1989. Ein System unecht-zylindrischer Kartennetze für Erdkarten. Kartographische Nachrichten, 39(3), 89–96.

Implementation by Bernie Jenny and Bojan Šavrič, Oregon State University, with a hint by Daniel Strebe, Mapthematics. Nov 30, 2014.

All projections are equal-area. Some parameter combinations break the projection into pieces. This is due to the difficulty of finding good initial guesses for the iterative computation of coordinates.
